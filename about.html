Protecting Charleston Nightlife

Exposing deceptive marketing and unlicensed adult entertainment in the Holy City.

Our Mission

Welcome to Bachelorette Charlston. We are a consumer watchdog group dedicated to ensuring that Charleston residents and visitors are not misled by deceptive entertainment brands. This information is gathered by locals in Charleston who know the exact zoning for bars and clubs. The ticket saves have been checked and addresses that are served. A local resident has phycially driven and checked credentials.

In recent years, Charleston has seen a rise in "pop-up" male revues that operate without proper licensing, bypass local safety ordinances, and use deceptive "bait-and-switch" marketing tactics to sell tickets for shows that do not exist as advertised.

Why This Matters

When "illegal" or unlicensed shows operate, they put consumers at risk by:

  • Lack of Security: Unlicensed venues often skip professional security protocols.
  • Deceptive Branding: Using famous brand names (like "Magic Mike" or "Chippendales") without authorization to sell low-quality tickets.
  • No Refund Recourse: Many of these entities operate under "shell" companies, making it impossible for customers to get their money back when a show is canceled or falsified.
Red Flags to Watch For
  • ⚠️ No Venue Listed: Tickets sold for "A Secret Location."
  • ⚠️ Generic Names: Using vague terms to avoid trademark infringement.
  • ⚠️ Stock Photos: Using professional photos that don't match the actual performers.

Deceptive Brands & Practices

Practice Description Status
Bait-and-Switch Advertising a "Hollywood Style" show but hosting it in a backyard or dive bar. Reported
Trademark Squatting Using confusingly similar names to established, licensed national tours. Under Review
